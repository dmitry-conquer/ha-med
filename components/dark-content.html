Our Senior Care Physical Therapy Services

Home Advantage Home Health Agency's in-home physical therapy helps seniors and adults rebuild strength, balance, and mobility after illness, injury, or surgery. Our licensed physical therapists design personalized plans that may include post-surgical rehab; gait, balance, and strength training; flexibility and endurance work; safe transfers; pain and swelling management; range-of-motion exercises; home safety assessments; and assistive device fitting.

With over 30 years of dedicated service and 24/7 availability, we're proud to offer our home health physical therapy services throughout Miami-Dade and Broward counties.
