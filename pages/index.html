Home Health Physical Therapy | In-Home PT for Seniors & Adults

When getting back on your feet feels overwhelming, in-home physical therapy brings expert care to you. In Miami-Dade and Broward counties, home health PT helps seniors and adults rebuild strength, balance, and mobility after illness, injury, or surgery; recover from joint replacement or stroke; manage pain; and prevent falls. Care is tailored to your goals with gait and balance training, strengthening, flexibility, endurance, safe transfers, and a clear home exercise plan.

If you're looking for licensed physical therapists, consider Home Advantage Home Health Agency. Our team delivers compassionate, evidence-based care at home across Miami-Dade and Broward counties. We create individualized plans, perform home safety assessments, fit and train on assistive devices, coach family caregivers, and coordinate with your physician and care team. With over 30 years of dedicated service, we schedule visits around your routine and help you move safely and confidently at home.

How Our Team Provides Physical
Therapy for the Elderly

Recovery feels easier when each visit meets you where you are. Guided by compassion and clinical expertise, our multilingual team of therapists tailors in-home physical therapy for older adults to fit your routines, your home, and your goals.

Here is how we support safer movement and greater independence:

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

After a hospital stay or joint replacement, we begin recovery at home, following your surgeon's protocol and monitoring vitals, swelling, and incision precautions. Sessions focus on gentle range-of-motion, progressive strengthening, gait and stair work, and safe transfers in your familiar spaces. You receive a clear home exercise plan, pacing guidance, caregiver coaching, and we update your physician to keep care aligned.
